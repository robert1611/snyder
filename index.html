AirBnB Alternatove

Welcome to our direct booking website. Tired of hidden costs like Airbnb fees, cleaning fees, convenience fees. We have simplified our pricing by giving you are per night costs, and that is it. Other advantages of direct booking: (1) ability to view the property before booking (2) reduced pricing by cutting out AirBnB (3) ability to measure distance between your worksite and properties and (4) easier communication since we can discuss or chat before booking.

Each calendar shows which dates are available because they are clear. If a date is unavailable, it will be shaded.

Reasons Why You Should Book With Us

If you're a windmill, solar, nurse or pipeline worker, or anyone working in the area for an extended time, finding the right place to stay can be a challenge. Hotels are expensive and lack the comfort of home, while unfurnished rentals require too much setup. Our fully furnished rental homes offer the perfect solution for long-term stays.

Our housing is conveniently located near major projects in Roscoe, Fluvanna, Borden County, Hermleigh, and Colorado City, making your commute quick and easy. Whether you're managing a wind farm, installing solar panels, or working on pipeline infrastructure, you'll have a comfortable and hassle-free place to stay while being close to the job site.

Unlike cramped hotels, our homes provide spacious living areas where crews can unwind after long shifts. The ample parking accommodates trucks and work vehicles with ease. Plus, with fully stocked kitchens, you can save money by cooking meals instead of eating out. Each of our properties has been recently remodeled with workers in mind. Enjoy the convenience of oversized washers and dryers, ideal for handling work clothes and gear.
